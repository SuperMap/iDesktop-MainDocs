Raster Statistics can help users to know the distribution of the raster values quickly, and can be used for further analysis. SuperMap provides 5 kinds of statistic modes: Basic Statistics, Zonal Statistics, Common Statistics, Altitude Statistics and Neighborhood Statistics.
